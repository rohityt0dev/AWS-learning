About Me


      

        

          

            I'm an entry-level DevOps Engineer based in Mumbai, India, focused on Infrastructure as Code,
            containerization, and CI/CD automation. I enjoy provisioning cloud infrastructure with Terraform,
            containerizing applications with Docker, and orchestrating them with Kubernetes — turning manual,
            error-prone processes into repeatable, version-controlled workflows.
          

          

            Why DevOps? I'm drawn to the discipline where software engineering meets systems
            reliability — where a single well-written script or Terraform module can eliminate hours of manual
            work and make deployments predictable. I want to keep building toward highly available, observable,
            and automated cloud systems.
          

          

            Career objective: To contribute to automation, CI/CD pipelines, and scalable cloud
            deployments within a collaborative engineering team, while continuously growing my expertise in
            AWS, Kubernetes, and platform reliability.
